# NL - Form - Date Picker component

The `NlDatePicker` component is part of the Form components redesign on the library.
It goes wrapped under the [NlFromWrapper](?path=/docs/components-forms-form-wrapper--overview) that share the information between the other components like `NlLabel` and `NlErrorMessage`.
