# NL - Accordion

`NlAccordion` wraps two components:

- The `NlAccordionTrigger` component handles the toggling of the accordion content to a visible/invisible state.
- The `NlAccordionContent` component is a wrapper of all the information show on the accordion.

For multiple accordions in succession, there is a component called `NlAccordionGroup`.
